Scenario 1 — Exit at Series A (~$15,400 · ~3× on $5K · ~2 years):
Your SAFE converts at the $6.5M cap when the Series A happens at an estimated $20M valuation. Because your cap is $6.5M, you get shares priced as if the company is worth far less than $20M — giving you a larger ownership slice than Series A investors paying full price.

Scenario 2 — Hold to Q4 2030 exit (~$38,500 · ~7.7× on $5K · ~4 years):
La Ruche reaches an estimated $50M valuation via Series B or strategic acquisition. Your $6.5M cap locked you in at today's early price — as the company grows from $6.5M to $50M, your stake grows with it.

Scenario 3 — Re-invest at Series A with pro-rata + 20% discount (~$54,000 total · 5.4× on $10K):
Investors of $25K or more unlock the right to re-invest at Series A at a 20% discount to the round price. Invest $5K now, re-invest $5K at Series A at the discounted rate, hold both to the $50M exit: $10K total invested → ~$54,000 out. The lower headline multiple reflects the higher capital deployed — the absolute return is the largest of all three scenarios.

First time angel investing? Common questions answered.
A SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) is the most common early-stage investment instrument. You invest money today and receive the right to convert it into equity (shares) when the company raises its next priced round (Series A). No interest, no repayment, no monthly payments. Your investment converts into shares at a discount to the Series A price. Created by Y Combinator — used by thousands of startups globally. Read YC's original SAFE documentation →
Your shares are calculated as if La Ruche is valued at $6.5M — regardless of the actual Series A valuation. If the Series A is at $20M, you get approximately 3× more shares than a Series A investor. The cap rewards you for investing early and taking more risk.
Angel investing is high risk. La Ruche is early-stage and may not reach its targets — you could lose your entire investment. Only invest what you can afford to lose. That said, La Ruche has institutional backing (Gates Foundation, Royal Academy of Engineering), regulatory certification, and real revenue — meaningful de-risking signals for this stage.
After signing via DocuSign, you receive wiring instructions for La Ruche Health's US bank account (Garden City, Idaho). Standard international wire — arrives in 1–3 business days. Crypto also accepted. You receive a confirmation and countersigned SAFE within 24 hours of receipt.
